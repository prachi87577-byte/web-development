India's Top Adventure Destinations

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Rishikesh is a fascinating blend of spirituality, adventure, and natural beauty nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas in Uttarakhand

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a stunning Himalayan resort town in Himachal Pradesh, beloved by backpackers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers alike. Nestled along the Beas River, it offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and thrilling activities—from skiing in Solang Valley to trekking in Parvati Valley, and even paragliding and rafting in the Pir Panjal mountains.

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Activities: Snorkeling, Scuba diving, Beach trekking, Kayaking

Best Time: October to May The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a stunning union territory of India, made up of 572 islands, of which only 38 are inhabited. They’re nestled between the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea, forming a tropical arc that stretches nearly 1,000 km between Myanmar and Indonesia.
